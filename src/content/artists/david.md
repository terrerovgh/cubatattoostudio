David is the lead artist and founder of Cuba Tattoo Studio. With over a decade of experience, he has mastered the art of hyper-realism and large-scale blackwork. His approach treats the body as a living canvas, where shadow and light play a crucial role in creating depth and dimension.

Specializing in portraiture and intricate dark surrealism, David's work is recognized for its high contrast and longevity. He works closely with each client to ensure that every piece is not just a tattoo, but a personal narrative etched in skin.
