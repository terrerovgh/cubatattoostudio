Karli is known for her bold use of color and strong lines, defining the Neo-Traditional style at Cuba Tattoo Studio. She reimagines classic motifs with a modern palette and dynamic composition, creating tattoos that are vibrant and impactful.

Her work often features stylized animals, floral arrangements, and lady faces, all executed with a high level of saturation and clean linework. Karli believes that a tattoo should look as good in 20 years as it does today, prioritizing readability and contrast in every design.
