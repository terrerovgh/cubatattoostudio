Nina brings a delicate touch to the studio, specializing in fine line and botanical tattoos. Her work is characterized by its elegance, precision, and flow, often incorporating organic elements that complement the body's natural curves.

With a background in illustration, Nina approaches each design with a focus on composition and subtlety. She excels at creating single-needle pieces that are both intricate and airy, perfect for those seeking a more refined aesthetic.
